# Common Influential Factors by Context

Comprehensive lists of frequently tested influential factors organized by business context.

## Email Marketing

### Subject Lines
- **Length:** Short (3-5 words) vs Medium (6-10) vs Long (11+)
- **Personalization:** Name, company, role, past behavior
- **Urgency:** Time-sensitive language vs evergreen
- **Curiosity:** Questions, incomplete thoughts, intrigue
- **Value proposition:** Benefit vs feature focused
- **Tone:** Formal vs casual vs playful
- **Numbers:** Including statistics vs avoiding them
- **Emojis:** Using vs not using, which ones

### From Name
- **Person vs company:** "John from Acme" vs "Acme Team"
- **Role clarity:** "CEO", "Support", generic
- **Personalization:** Sender name matching recipient relationship

### Preview Text
- **Relationship to subject:** Complementing vs repeating vs extending
- **Length:** Short vs utilizing full space
- **CTA inclusion:** Yes vs no

### Send Time
- **Time of day:** Early morning, mid-day, evening, night
- **Day of week:** Weekday patterns, weekend
- **Timezone:** Sender vs recipient timezone
- **Frequency:** Daily, weekly, monthly, triggered

### Email Body
- **Format:** Plain text vs HTML vs hybrid
- **Length:** Brief vs detailed
- **Image ratio:** Image-heavy vs text-heavy vs balanced
- **Personalization:** Dynamic content depth
- **CTA count:** Single vs multiple
- **CTA design:** Button vs link vs both
- **Social proof:** Customer logos, testimonials, statistics

## Landing Pages

### Headlines
- **Approach:** Benefit vs feature vs question vs statement
- **Length:** Short (5-7 words) vs long (10+)
- **Subheadline:** Present vs absent, approach

### Hero Section
- **Image type:** People, product, abstract, lifestyle
- **Media format:** Static image vs video vs animation
- **CTA prominence:** Size, color, position
- **Copy density:** Minimal vs comprehensive

### Social Proof
- **Type:** Testimonials, customer logos, statistics, ratings
- **Placement:** Top, middle, bottom, multiple locations
- **Credibility indicators:** Photos, names, companies, specifics
- **Format:** Quotes, carousel, grid, video

### Forms
- **Field count:** Minimal (2-3) vs standard (5-7) vs extensive (10+)
- **Field order:** Logical flow variations
- **Required indicators:** Asterisk, "(required)", none
- **Inline validation:** Real-time vs submit-time
- **Progressive disclosure:** All at once vs multi-step
- **Labels:** Above vs inline vs placeholder

### Trust Signals
- **Security badges:** SSL, payment processors, certifications
- **Guarantees:** Money-back, satisfaction, specific promises
- **Privacy statements:** Prominent vs standard
- **Third-party validation:** Awards, press mentions, associations

### Layout
- **Column structure:** Single vs multi-column
- **Visual hierarchy:** Primary CTA emphasis
- **White space:** Generous vs compact
- **Above-the-fold:** CTA visible vs scroll-required

## In-Product Experience

### Onboarding
- **Step count:** Minimal (1-3) vs standard (4-7) vs extensive (8+)
- **Information density:** Per step
- **Progress indication:** Steps, bar, percentage, none
- **Skip options:** Available vs required completion
- **Tutorial style:** Tooltips, modals, tours, video, none
- **Timing:** Immediate vs deferred vs just-in-time

### Empty States
- **Messaging tone:** Encouraging, informative, playful
- **Suggested actions:** Clear next steps vs exploration
- **Example content:** Show possibilities
- **Visual design:** Illustration vs text-only

### Feature Discovery
- **Method:** Tooltips, product tours, just-in-time, none
- **Trigger timing:** Immediate, after action, time-delayed
- **Dismissibility:** Permanent, temporary, persistent
- **Highlight style:** Overlay, pointer, subtle

### Navigation
- **Structure:** Hierarchical depth
- **Labels:** Feature names, benefit-focused, icons+text
- **Placement:** Top, side, hamburger
- **Search prominence:** Always visible vs hidden

### Notifications
- **Frequency:** Real-time, batched, digest
- **Tone:** Urgent vs informative vs friendly
- **Actionability:** Direct action vs informational
- **Channels:** In-app, email, push, SMS
- **Customization:** User control level

### Error Messages
- **Tone:** Technical vs plain language vs friendly
- **Specificity:** Generic vs detailed guidance
- **Resolution help:** How to fix prominently shown
- **Contact options:** Support visibility

## Checkout & Conversion Flows

### Form Design
- **Field count:** Minimize vs collect comprehensive data
- **Field labels:** Descriptive vs minimal
- **Error messages:** Inline vs summary vs both
- **Auto-fill support:** Address, payment, contact
- **Optional fields:** Clearly marked vs hidden
- **Field validation:** Real-time vs on submit

- **Security indicators:** Lock icons, SSL badge, secure checkout badge
- **Payment options:** Display all vs select-to-show
- **Return policy:** Prominent vs link vs hidden
- **Shipping information:** Upfront vs at checkout
- **Contact visibility:** Phone, chat, email prominence

- **Account requirement:** Required vs optional vs guest

- **Registration timing:** Before vs after purchase
- **Save for later:** Available vs not

## Usage Notes

**Selecting Factors:**
- Start with factors specific to your business context
- Consider what you can realistically test given your traffic
- Prioritize factors that align with user feedback or data anomalies

**Combining Factors:**
- Test independently first to establish baselines
- Then test winning combinations
- Watch for interaction effects

**Context Matters:**
- What works in one context may not work in another
- Your audience, product, and brand all influence results
- Use these as starting points, not guarantees
