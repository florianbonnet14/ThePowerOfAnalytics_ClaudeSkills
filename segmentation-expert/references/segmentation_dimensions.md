# Segmentation Dimensions Catalog

## Demographic Dimensions

### B2C
- Age groups (18-24, 25-34, 35-44, 45-54, 55+)
- Gender
- Income level (quartiles or brackets)
- Education level
- Geographic location (country, region, city, urban/suburban/rural)
- Language
- Household composition (single, couple, family with kids)
- Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)

### B2B
- Company size by employees (<50, 50-200, 200-1000, 1000+)
- Company size by revenue (<$1M, $1-10M, $10-50M, $50M+)
- Industry/vertical (SaaS, E-commerce, Healthcare, Finance, etc.)
- Geographic location (country, region)
- Company age (<1 year, 1-3 years, 3-10 years, 10+ years)
- Funding stage (Bootstrap, Seed, Series A-B, Series C+)
- Business model (B2B, B2C, B2B2C, Marketplace)

## Behavioral Dimensions

### Usage-Based
- Activity level (Power: >20 sessions/mo, Regular: 10-20, Casual: 1-9, Inactive: 0)
- Feature adoption (# features used, specific feature usage)
- Session frequency (daily, weekly, monthly active users)
- Session duration (short, medium, long sessions)
- Time of day usage (morning, afternoon, evening, night)
- Day of week usage (weekday vs weekend)
- Depth of usage (pages/screens viewed, actions taken)

### Transaction-Based
- Purchase frequency (first-time, occasional, frequent, loyal)
- Order value (low, medium, high, very high)
- Product categories purchased (cross-sell patterns)
- Payment method (credit card, PayPal, invoice, etc.)
- Discount usage (never, occasional, always looks for deals)
- Cart abandonment behavior

### Engagement-Based
- Email engagement (open rate, click rate)
- Support interaction (never contacted, occasional, frequent)
- Community participation (lurker, contributor, leader)
- Content consumption (reader, watcher, listener)
- Social sharing (never shares, occasional, brand advocate)
- Feedback provision (never, survey responder, active feedback)

## Acquisition Dimensions

- **Channel:** Organic search, Paid search, Social (organic), Social (paid), Email, Referral, Partnership, Direct, PR, Content
- **Campaign source:** Specific campaign IDs
- **Landing page:** Which page first landed on
- **Referrer domain:** Where they came from
- **Attribution model:** First touch, last touch, multi-touch
- **UTM parameters:** Source, medium, campaign, content, term

## Lifecycle Dimensions

- **Customer age (tenure):** <1 month, 1-3 months, 3-6 months, 6-12 months, 1+ year
- **Cohort:** Grouped by signup month/quarter/year
- **Lifecycle stage:** 
  - Trial
  - New active (first 30-90 days)
  - Active
  - At-risk (declining engagement)
  - Churned
  - Resurrected (came back after churn)
- **Product tier:** Free, Basic, Pro, Enterprise
- **Upgrade/downgrade history:** Never changed, upgraded, downgraded, churned and returned

## Psychographic Dimensions

- **Job to be done (JTBD):** Primary use case
- **Goals/motivations:** What they want to achieve
- **Pain points:** Problems they're solving
- **Persona:** Research-based archetypes
- **Purchase decision criteria:** Price, features, support, brand, etc.
- **Technology adoption:** Early adopter, mainstream, laggard

## Firmographic Dimensions (B2B)

- **Industry vertical:** Tech, Healthcare, Finance, Retail, Manufacturing, etc.
- **Revenue range:** <$1M, $1-10M, $10-50M, $50-100M, $100M+
- **Employee count:** 1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 201-1000, 1000+
- **Technology stack:** Tools and platforms used
- **Decision-maker role:** C-level, VP, Director, Manager, Individual Contributor
- **Budget size:** Low, medium, high
- **Procurement process:** Simple (credit card), complex (procurement, legal)
- **Geographic presence:** Single location, multi-location, international

## Combining Dimensions

### Common 2D Segmentations
- Plan Tier × Engagement Level
- Acquisition Channel × Lifecycle Stage
- Company Size × Industry
- Feature Usage × Tenure
- Value Tier × At-Risk Status

### Common 3D Segmentations
- Plan Tier × Engagement × Tenure
- Channel × Value × Lifecycle Stage
- Company Size × Industry × Product Tier
